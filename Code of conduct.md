# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers of MedInsight AI pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the patients this tool ultimately serves
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Sharing patient data or personally identifiable health information in issues or PRs
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Clinical Responsibility

Contributors must not introduce changes that could:
- Cause the system to provide misleading clinical information without appropriate disclaimers
- Remove safety warnings or physician-use-only notices
- Enable the tool to be used as a standalone diagnostic system without physician oversight

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
